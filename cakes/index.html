Frequently asked questions

  • Can I order a customized cake?

    • Yes, you can customize your cake, as we have photo cakes, where you can upload the best clicked photo and customize it accordingly.

    • Yes, we do deliver eggless cakes. All the displayed cakes are available in eggless variant as well. You need to choose “Make it eggless” Option available on product detail page. Be assured about the purity of eggless cakes. They are 100% vegetarian cakes with delicious taste.

  • Does your website also deliver on Sundays and public holidays?

    • Cakes Bakery works on all Sundays and holidays except under uncontrollable situations like bad weather or civil disorder like bandhs.
